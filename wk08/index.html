    Future Value
        Future Value - value of asset at a specific date (future date ??)
        Future Value Single Sum
            Rate - interest / number of periods (ex: 6% / 4 quarterly payments)
            Nper - number of payment periods
            Pmt  - payment made each period. Does NOT change over the time of the loan
            PV   - present value
            Type - cash flow at end of period, or beginning of next period
    Future Value Single Sum Calculations
        Future Value calculations
    Future Value - Positive and Negative Numbers
        Positive - if the PV (present value) is positive the FV will be negative
        Negative - vice versa of above
    Future Value Future Single Sum with Quarterly Compounding 
        ex: if you DEPOSIT $100 in an account earning 6 percent interest for 5 years with interest compounding quarterly:
           Nper = 20 (5 years * 4 quarterly interest payments)
           Rate = .015 (6% interest / 4 quarterly interest payments)
    Future Value Future Single Sum with Monthly Compounding
        ex: $100 DEPOSIT at monthly compounding interest     
            Nper = 60 (5 years * 12 monthly interest payments)
            Rate = .005 (6% interest / 12 monthly interest payments)
    Present Value
        Present Value (PV) aka 'Discounted Value' = measures worth of money or stream of payments adjusted for interest and inflation
    Present Value Single Sum Calculation    
        ex: you RECEIVE $100 one year from now, what is the PV of $100 if you use a 6% interest or discount rate?
            the Final Value (FV) = $100, there is 6% interest (.06) with 1 pay period (Nper)
            the PV = $94.34 
    Present Value Single Sum Calculation - 5 Year
        ex: RECEIVE $100 in five years, what is the PV @ 6% interest?
            the FV = $100, there 6% interest with 5 pay periods (Nper)
            the PV = $74.73
    Present Value Single Sum Calculation - 15 Years
        ex: RECEIVE $1000 in 15 years, what is the PV @ 7% interest?
            FV = $1000, Nper = 15, Rate = .07
            PV = $362.45 
    Interest Rate 
        Interest Rate - stated at percent, amount charged to borrower for using lender's money (principal) 
        Interest Rate Calculations - return the interest rate per period of loan or investment. 
    Interest Rate Calculation
        ex: If you sold land for $11,933 that you bought for $5,000 5 years ago, what would your annual rate of return be?
            FV = 11,933, Nper = 5, Rate = ?
            PV = -5,000
            the rate comes out to 19%
    Number of Periods (NPER)
        Number of Periods = returns the number of payment periods for a loan
    Payments

    Effective Interest Rate
        Effective Interest Rate - amount paid on loan due to compounding interest over time
